About Vibe Simulation

VibeSimulation.com was born from a passion for both physics and the beautiful mathematics animations of Manim. Our mission is to make complex, abstract concepts in science tangible and intuitive. We believe that visualization is a powerful tool for learning and that anyone can grasp even the most challenging topics with the right visual aids.

Our creator, a physicist and software engineer, meticulously crafts each 2-3 minute animation to be accurate, clear, and engaging. We focus on building a strong foundation and then exploring the frontiers of modern physics, one animation at a time.
