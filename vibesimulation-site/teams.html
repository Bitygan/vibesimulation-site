For teams

A stronger way to show model behavior than a slide deck alone.

The labs are useful when a team needs to communicate a dynamic system, compare parameter regimes, or preserve a better trail of what was actually shown during a demo or review.

Team use cases
  • Scenario communication. Show cause and effect live instead of describing it abstractly.
  • Technical storytelling. Make a model more legible to non-specialists without flattening it into marketing.
  • Review trail. Keep a verifiable record of the settings and interactions used during a session.
